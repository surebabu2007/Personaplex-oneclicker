FIRST TIME SETUP:
-----------------
1. Double-click: INSTALL_PERSONAPLEX.bat
2. Follow the prompts to complete installation
3. When asked, enter your HuggingFace token
   (Get one at: https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens)

RUNNING PERSONAPLEX:
-------------------
After installation, just double-click: START_PERSONAPLEX.bat

Then open your browser to: https://localhost:8998

PUBLIC SHARE (SHAREABLE LINK):
-----------------------------
Double-click: START_PERSONAPLEX_PUBLIC.bat
It will print a public HTTPS link in the terminal.
Keep the window open to keep the link active.

REQUIREMENTS:
-------------
- Windows 10 or 11
- Python 3.10 or higher (https://python.org)
- NVIDIA GPU with 12GB+ VRAM (RTX 3080 or better recommended)
- Internet connection (first run downloads ~14GB of models)

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
- Out of memory? Use: START_PERSONAPLEX_CPU_OFFLOAD.bat
- Installation issues? See: INSTALL.md
- Check your setup: CHECK_STATUS.bat
- Full documentation: README.md
